# SoulPass — Brand

A dark, neon-accented identity for an on-chain reputation product targeting Gen Z event-goers and organizers. Funky, playful, and trustworthy at the same time. Inspired by Myntra FWD's bold expressive cards.

## Core palette
- `--bg`: `#08090A` — near-black, slight blue undertone
- `--surface`: `#101113` — primary card surface
- `--surface-2`: `#16181B` — elevated card / row surface
- `--border`: `#1F2228` — hairline borders
- `--text`: `#F6F7F8` — primary text on dark
- `--muted`: `#7A828C` — secondary text
- `--accent`: `#B5FF1A` — signature lime/chartreuse (from logo)
- `--accent-soft`: `#1B240B` — accent at low opacity for chips/cards
- `--positive`: `#7AFFB6` — confirmations
- `--warn`: `#FFC34D` — warnings
- `--danger`: `#FF5C7A` — destructive

## Typography
- Display: **Space Grotesk** — bold-extrabold for hero numbers, titles, and accent stats. The "wide-cut" Web3-game vibe seen in the Figma.
- UI: **Inter** — body and most UI labels, default 14–16px.
- Mono: **JetBrains Mono** — addresses, transaction signatures, counters.

## Voice
- Punchy. One verb per CTA: **Register**, **Check In**, **Connect**, **Rate**.
- No blockchain jargon in copy. Never say "wallet", "gas", "mint", "transaction" to users.
- Reputation framed as growth: "+10 rep", "Streak 3", "Top 5% Networker".

## Visual rules
- Generous corner radii — chips/buttons `rounded-full`, cards `rounded-3xl`.
- Lime accent is rare; reserve it for primary CTAs, the active reputation ring, and the brand wordmark dot.
- Dark cards on dark backgrounds with low-opacity hairline borders, never heavy shadows.
- Big numbers are the hero — reputation score, attendees, badges all lean huge.
